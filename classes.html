Your Classes


Now, I'm going to take you through the classes features. On the option bar, there is a feature called Classes. Near it there is a drop down where you can see all your classes for the semester. When you tap on one class, you can see all the details of that class all in one place. In a class page, you can see your classmates, teachers, attendance and timetables (as shown in the images below). You can also see messages sent by your teacher but they also come onto your Gmail. Lastly, you can see your unit details (the ATL, SOI, Unit etc.) and the callendar that you can also see on the Dashboard.
